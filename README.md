# Neon Dodge - Extreme Edition

**Neon Dodge** is a fast-paced, neon-styled dodging game built for desktop browsers. Navigate your glowing player through an endless stream of obstacles and challenge yourself to achieve the highest score possible!

## Features

- **Dark & Neon Theme:** Stunning neon graphics and glowing effects for a visually immersive experience.
- **Optimized Gameplay:** Smooth performance with optimized obstacle spawning and particle effects.
- **Stage-based Difficulty:** Obstacle speed increases every 30 points for progressively harder gameplay.
- **Dynamic Obstacles:** Each spawn includes 3-4 obstacles with varying sizes and optional lateral movement.
- **Score Tracking:** Keep track of your score and high score in real-time.
- **Particle Effects:** Explosions and neon particles enhance the visual feedback when obstacles collide.

## Controls

- **Move Left:** `Arrow Left` or `A`
- **Move Right:** `Arrow Right` or `D`

## Notes

- This game is **designed for desktop browsers only**. Mobile play is not supported.
- For the best experience, use a modern browser with JavaScript enabled.

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your desktop browser.
2. Use the arrow keys or A/D to move your player left and right.
3. Avoid obstacles and try to survive as long as possible.
